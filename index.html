About game

Explore different gameplay styles! Each hero in Drakantos stands out with their unique mechanics, personality traits, and story roles. Breaking away from traditional MMORPG class systems, our heroes bring a new approach to cooperative gameplay. With each update, we will introduce new heroes, ensuring an ever-evolving landscape of strategies and dynamics for players to explore and dominate.

Form teams with your friends, other adventurers or even go alone and explore dynamic dungeons in order to evolve your heroes, obtain rare items and better equipment. These missions change over time in the game, introducing a new range of enemies, bosses, resources, traps and objectives. During the dungeon there are chances of special events and twists, making each experience unique and challenging.

Drakantos offers more than a cooperative journey. For those looking to test their skills against other players, our PvP system provides the perfect battleground. With a diversity of game modes, a ranked duel system and regular tournaments, the opportunity to become a champion in our online community is within your reach! In PvP, only skills and strategies matter; everyone's attributes will be equalized to ensure a competitive environment.

Drakantos isn't just a place for combat; It's also a stage to express your unique style! Unlock exclusive icons, cool titles and skins to further customize your profile and heroes. Collect dozens of charming pets and liven up your interactions with more than 30 emotes per hero, each with unique lines!

  • Skill Customization: Try different play styles with the same hero, modifying their abilities with orbs and artifacts.
  • Stream System: Stream your game progress or watch others in real time!
  • Houses:Get your own house and decorate it your way.
  • Market:Trade rare items, collectibles, and more with other players.
  • And much more:Highscores, Day and Night Cycles, Crafting, etc.
